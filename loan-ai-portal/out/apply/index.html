Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

Our AI-powered experience delivers a 2-minute pre-qualification and rate check with only a soft credit pull, so there is no impact on your score. If you choose an offer and complete the lender’s underwriting, final approval and funding can follow shortly after.

Will checking my rate affect my credit score?

Checking your rate triggers only a soft credit inquiry, so there is no impact on your credit score. A hard inquiry happens only if you accept an offer and finish the full application with a lender.

What can I use the loan for?

Personal loans through our platform can fund debt consolidation, home improvements, medical expenses, major purchases, weddings, vacations, or nearly any personal financial need.

What if I have bad credit?

We partner with lenders who work with every credit tier, including fair and poor credit. Our AI matching engine highlights lenders most likely to approve your application given your credit profile.

Are there any fees?

Our matching service is completely free. Individual lenders may charge origination or other fees, and those will always be disclosed before you accept any loan offer.
